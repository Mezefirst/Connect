About EthioGlobal Connect

Founded in 2025, EthioGlobal Connect emerged from a passion to unite Ethiopians across borders. Our platform is built on cultural preservation, community empowerment, and global collaboration principles. We believe in the power of our diaspora to drive positive change in Ethiopia and our adopted homes worldwide.
